Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Kelsi

A proud little dirt baby straight outta southern Mississippi — grew up knee-deep in red clay and big dreams. At 20, I hauled myself to Virginia for a long, satisfying run in kitchen and bathroom design. Then at 34, West Virginia whispered "come play in the real dirt," and here I am — studio built, kiln humming, and finally throwing pots like it's the best day ever.
